Frequently Asked Questions

Select an app below to see relevant FAQs.

What is Dashboard AIR?
Dashboard AIR is an AI routing app for iPhone and iPad. You type a prompt once and the app recommends the best AI tool for your task — whether that's ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Grok, and more. One prompt. The right AI. Every time.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel: open Settings on your iPhone or iPad → tap your name → Subscriptions → tap Dashboard AIR → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find Dashboard AIR, and select "Request a refund."
What's included in the Pro plan?
Pro unlocks routing across all AI tools and categories, confidence scoring, custom category pinning, theme customization, prompt history, and the AI Advisor monthly report. Free users get 3 routes per day across the top three tools.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Sign in with the same email you used to subscribe. Your Pro or Teams access will restore automatically. If it doesn't appear, go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases."
Why do I need to verify my email?
Email verification is required to access Dashboard AIR. After signing up, check your inbox for a verification link from us. Tap the link, return to the app, and tap "I've verified my email." If you didn't receive the email, check your spam folder or use the resend option on the verification screen.
How do I delete my account?
Open Dashboard AIR → tap the gear icon (⚙️) → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data.
The app says "routing offline" — what does that mean?
This means the app couldn't reach our AI routing engine. Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, contact us at support@4cylindertechnologies.com.
Is my data private?
Yes. Dashboard AIR does not store your prompt content on our servers. Routing requests are processed and discarded. Your account information is stored securely in Firebase. We do not sell your data to any third party. Read our full Privacy Policy.
What is Ask AIR?
Ask AIR is a conversational AI command center coming soon from 4Cylinder Technologies. It executes prompts across multiple AI engines natively — delivering answers inside the app without routing you elsewhere. Stay tuned for the launch announcement.
When will Ask AIR be available?
Ask AIR is currently in development. Follow us for updates or contact support@4cylindertechnologies.com to be notified at launch.
What is HelmIQ?
HelmIQ is an AI-powered home inventory app in development. Photograph your belongings and AI catalogs, locates, and manages them for you — built for homeowners, renters, and anyone who wants to know what they own and where it is.
When will HelmIQ be available?
HelmIQ is currently in development. Contact support@4cylindertechnologies.com to be notified when it launches.
