Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dashboard AIR?
Dashboard AIR is an AI routing app for iPhone and iPad. You type a prompt once and the app recommends the best AI tool for your task — whether that's ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Grok, and more. One prompt. The right AI. Every time.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel: open Settings on your iPhone or iPad → tap your name → Subscriptions → tap Dashboard AIR → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find Dashboard AIR, and select "Request a refund."
What's included in the Pro plan?
Pro unlocks routing across all AI categories, confidence scoring, custom category pinning, theme customization, and priority support. Free users get access to the top three categories with standard routing and 3 routes per day.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Sign in with the same email you used to subscribe. Your Pro or Teams access will restore automatically. If it doesn't appear, go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases."
How do I delete my account?
Open Dashboard AIR → tap the gear icon (⚙️) → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete Account. You'll be asked to confirm. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data.
The app says "routing offline" — what does that mean?
This means the app couldn't reach our AI routing engine. Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, contact us at michael.welton@4cylindertechnologies.com.
Is my data private?
Yes. Dashboard AIR does not store your prompt content on our servers. Routing requests are processed and discarded. Your account information is stored securely in Firebase. We do not sell your data to any third party. Read our full Privacy Policy.
