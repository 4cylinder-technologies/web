Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026 · Applies to all 4Cylinder Technologies apps

Overview

4Cylinder Technologies LLC ("4Cylinder," "we," "us," or "our") is a Delaware-incorporated software company operating out of Charlotte, NC. We build mobile applications designed to make AI work harder for you.

This Privacy Policy applies to all apps published by 4Cylinder Technologies LLC, including DashboardAIR, AskAIR, and MyOwnIQ. It describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

We believe in plain language. This policy is written to be read — not to bury what matters in legal jargon.

Apps Covered by This Policy

DashboardAIR

The AI power user command center. Type one prompt — DashboardAIR routes it to the best AI tool automatically, renders the answer in-app, and shows the confidence score. Available on iOS and Android.

AskAIR

The consumer AI guide. Tap a life category, ask your question, get an answer from the right AI — all inside the app. No switching, no copy-pasting. Available on iOS and Android.

An AI-powered home inventory app. Photograph your belongings — AI catalogs, locates, and manages them. Currently in open beta.

What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our services.

  • Account information — your email address, used to create and manage your account.
  • Subscription status — your plan tier (Free, Pro, Teams, or Family), managed through Apple App Store or Google Play via RevenueCat.
  • Usage data — anonymized routing history used to improve our AI routing engine. We do not store the full content of your prompts on our servers beyond what is required to generate your answer.
  • Device information — device type and OS version, collected automatically to ensure compatibility.
  • Scan photos (MyOwnIQ) — photos you take to catalog items are sent securely to our AI provider for identification, then discarded. They are never stored on our servers and never used to train AI models.
  • Inventory data (MyOwnIQ) — the items, locations, and values in your inventory sync to your private account, encrypted at rest and in transit. Visible only to you, and to family members you explicitly invite to a shared property.

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, contacts, or any sensitive personal information unless explicitly required for a specific feature and disclosed at the time of collection.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your data to any third party — ever.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not store the full content of your AI prompts on our servers.
  • We do not share your personal information with AI providers — only your prompt is passed to generate your answer.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Security

We take security seriously. Here is how we protect your data:

🔒 Biometric Authentication

DashboardAIR, AskAIR, and MyOwnIQ support Face ID, Touch ID, and Android biometric unlock. MyOwnIQ also offers Secure Location, which lets you lock sensitive properties, areas, or spots behind biometric authentication — a lock applied to a parent location is inherited by everything inside it, and a lock you set directly on a specific location can only be removed there, never by a parent. When enabled, biometric data never leaves your device — it is processed entirely by your device's operating system (iOS Secure Enclave / Android Keystore). We never have access to your biometric data.

🔐 Data Encryption

Sensitive data stored on your device — including authentication tokens and subscription status — is encrypted at rest using platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) via expo-secure-store. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher.

🛡️ Session Security

Authentication sessions are managed securely via Firebase Authentication. The app supports automatic session timeout after a period of inactivity. Clipboard data containing copied prompts is automatically cleared after use to protect your privacy.

🔑 API Key Security

All AI service API keys are stored in Google Cloud Secret Manager — never in app code or client-side storage. Keys are encrypted at rest and access-controlled at the infrastructure level.

AIR Consensus Engine™

DashboardAIR and AskAIR use the AIR Consensus Engine™ to determine which AI tool is best suited for each routing category. On a monthly basis, our automated system polls leading AI models — including Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Grok, Gemini, and Microsoft Copilot — asking each to evaluate the best tool for each category. Results are scored, reviewed, and published to our routing system.

During this process, no personal user data is transmitted. The polling queries are generic category-level questions with no user identifiers, prompt content, or account information attached. The AIR Consensus Engine™ operates entirely at the system level — it does not access or process individual user prompts or history.

Routing assignments determined by the AIR Consensus Engine™ are updated monthly and apply universally to all users. You can learn more about how this works at 4cylindertechnologies.com.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Unverified accounts may be automatically removed after 72 hours. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

You have the right to:

Biometric Data

When you enable biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or Android biometrics), your biometric data is processed entirely on your device by the operating system. 4Cylinder Technologies LLC never receives, stores, or has access to your biometric data in any form. Biometric authentication can be disabled at any time in Settings.

Children's Privacy

None of our apps are directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

AskAIR carries an age rating of 13+ (Apple App Store) / Everyone (Google Play) and is not intended for use by anyone under 13. DashboardAIR and MyOwnIQ carry a general-audience rating (4+ Apple App Store / Everyone Google Play); a general-audience store rating means the app's content is appropriate for all ages, not that the app is designed for or marketed to children. All three apps require an account with a verified email address, and we do not knowingly collect account information, scan photos, or inventory data from anyone we know to be under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account or provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@4cylindertechnologies.com and we will delete it.
