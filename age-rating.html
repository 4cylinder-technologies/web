Age Ratings & Content Guidance

Know before
you download.

Each app we build has a different content profile, so each one carries its own age rating. Here's exactly what that means and why it differs across our apps.

Ratings by App

Three apps. Three ratings.

Age ratings reflect what each app actually does — not a one-size-fits-all default. MyOwnIQ is a self-contained camera and inventory tool. DashboardAIR and AskAIR route your prompts out to third-party AI models we don't control the output of, which changes the rating picture.

MyOwnIQ™

✓ 4+ / Everyone
App Store: 4+ Google Play: Everyone

MyOwnIQ uses your camera to identify and catalog items you photograph in your own spaces. There's no chat interface, no open AI conversation, no social feed, and no user-generated content shared publicly or with other users.

  • No unrestricted web or AI chat access
  • No social features or public content sharing
  • Camera used only to scan your own belongings

DashboardAIR™

17+ / Mature
App Store: 17+ Google Play: Mature 17+

DashboardAIR routes your prompts to third-party AI models (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Grok) selected by the AIR Consensus Engine™. Responses come directly from those providers and are not pre-filtered or moderated by us before display.

  • Unrestricted, unfiltered AI-generated responses
  • Open-ended prompts can surface mature or unpredictable content
  • Not intended for use by children

AskAIR™

17+ / Mature
App Store: 17+ Google Play: Mature 17+

AskAIR is a consumer-facing guide over the same underlying AI routing as DashboardAIR. Same reasoning applies: answers are generated by third-party AI providers we don't control, across open-ended topics including health, money, and more.

  • Unrestricted, unfiltered AI-generated responses
  • Open-ended prompts across sensitive life topics
  • Not intended for use by children

Why the difference? Age ratings are based on what an app can actually surface to a user, not just its category. MyOwnIQ is a closed-loop camera tool — it only ever shows you information about photos you took. DashboardAIR and AskAIR are AI routers: they hand your prompt to an outside AI model and display whatever that model returns, unedited. That's the same reason general-purpose AI chat apps are typically rated 17+/Mature on both app stores.

Parents: if you're setting up Screen Time, Family Sharing, or Google Family Link controls, please rely on the age rating shown in each app's official App Store or Google Play listing as the authoritative source — this page is a plain-language explainer, not a replacement for the store listing.
