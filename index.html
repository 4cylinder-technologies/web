Introducing

AIR Consensus Engine™
No bias. Best tool always wins.

Most AI routers pick favorites. Ours doesn't. The AIR Consensus Engine™ polls the world's top AI models every month — Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Grok — and asks each one a simple question: which AI tool is objectively best for this category? Majority rules. The winner routes your prompts. No politics, no contracts, no bias.

STEP 01
Poll the Top AI Models

Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Grok each vote on the best tool for every routing category — including tools that don't exist yet.

STEP 02
Score the Consensus

Votes are tallied. Majority wins. Split decisions are flagged for human review before going live. Confidence levels are tracked.

STEP 03
Update Instantly

Routing assignments update live — no app release required. As AI evolves, your router evolves with it. Automatically. Every month.

"Routing assignments determined by AI consensus — not by us. Every month, the world's top AI tools vote on who wins each category."
